Pro tip · Dica

Stuck? Try "This is the feeling when…" or "You do this when…" or just act it out. The weirder your description, the more you both remember it.

Free conversation · Conversa livre

Put the scripts away. Say ONE real thing to each other completely in the other's language. It can be anything: "Your pronunciation is better than mine." "This is harder than I thought." "I'm hungry." Just go.

No reveals. No scripts. The other person responds naturally. That's the whole game.
